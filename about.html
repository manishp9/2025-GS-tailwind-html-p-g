Barry Perlstein

CEO & Founder

As the founder of a multi-office insurance conglomerate, Barry has
become lauded as an insurance encyclopedia, having personally
scoured the globe for effective coverage solutions for his clients.
Beyond his desire to overdeliver, his honest approach and positive
persona are admired by every one of his 100+ employees.

An entity that has morphed into a sizable operation, P&G’s growth has
evolved along with the acquisition of multiple independent entities, with
its latest location in Miami, Florida in 2024.

“I started my business as a solo entrepreneur and realized the value of
truly being in tune with our employees’ best interests because we’re
only as effective as our network of staff members.” Using this
methodology as grounds for a unique company culture, there’s a
dedication at P&G that’s felt and appreciated by our clients.

A well-established figure in the healthcare insurance niche, David has over a

Landing his role and reputation as a healthcare insurance expert, David gives

“I used to have a hard time delegating, and still do, to some extent, but I

committed to entry-level clients and high-profile accounts.
